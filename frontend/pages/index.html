Youth Diabetes Crisis

Youth diabetes in the United States has increased significantly over the past few decades. According to the CDC, 1 in 3 teens aged 12-17—totaling 8.4 million young people—have prediabetes as of 2023. Type 2 diabetes cases are projected to increase by approximately 700% by the year 2060, affecting more young people than ever before.
