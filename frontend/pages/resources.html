Resources for Youth Diabetes

A curated list of trusted resources to help manage and understand diabetes in children and adolescents.

Statistics & Data

CDC National Diabetes Statistics Report

The primary source for diabetes prevalence data in the United States, including youth-specific statistics.

Youth Diabetes On the Rise

New Research Uncovers Concerning Increases in Youth Living with Diabetes in the U.S.

IDF Diabetes Atlas

Global facts and figures regarding diabetes prevalence from the International Diabetes Federation.

SEARCH for Diabetes in Youth

A multicenter study tracking the incidence and prevalence of diabetes in children and adolescents.

Prediabetes & Diabetes Care

Breakthrough T1D (formerly JDRF)

Leading global organization for Type 1 diabetes research, advocacy, and support for newly diagnosed families.

Beyond Type 1 - School Resources

Guides for managing diabetes in educational settings, including 504 plans and teacher training.

NIDDK - Preventing Type 2 in Kids

National Institute of Health resources focused on pediatric prediabetes and Type 2 prevention.

Healthy Diet & Nutrition

ADA Diabetes Food Hub

A comprehensive digital library of diabetes-friendly recipes and meal planning tools.

The Diabetes Plate Method

A simple, visual approach to portion control and balanced meal creation.

Physical Activity

ADA - Fitness & Diabetes

Safety guidelines for staying active while managing blood sugar levels.

Diabetes UK - Kids & Activity

Practical tips for young people on how different types of exercise affect glucose levels.

Non-Profit Organizations & Support

The Diabetes Link

Peer-based support specifically for teens and young adults transitioning to college and adulthood.

Children's Diabetes Foundation

Supports patient care and research at the Barbara Davis Center for Diabetes.

Diversity in Diabetes

Dedicated to awareness and resources for underrepresented communities in diabetes care.

The diaTribe Foundation

Provides insights and education to help people make sense of diabetes technology and treatment.
